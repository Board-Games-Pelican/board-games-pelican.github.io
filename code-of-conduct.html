Code of Conduct

  • Be Respectful: Treat everyone with courtesy, regardless of skill level, background, or experience.
  • Play Fair: Honor the rules of the games and play with integrity. No cheating or unsportsmanlike behavior.
  • Encourage Learning: Support beginners and share knowledge generously. We’re here to grow together.
  • Keep It Friendly: Maintain a positive atmosphere. No trash talk, gloating, or disruptive behavior.
  • Respect Time & Space: Be mindful of others waiting to play. Clean up after yourself and respect the venue.
  • Zero Tolerance for Harassment: Any form of discrimination, harassment, or intimidation will not be tolerated.

Let’s make this meetup a great experience for everyone!
